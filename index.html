About the Conference

ICSPR-2019 is being organized with a vision to address the various issues to promote the creation of intelligent solutions in future. It is expected that researchers will bring new prospects for collaboration across disciplines and gain ideas facilitating novel concepts. The theme of this conference is to motivate the researchers to adopt the outcome for implementation. ICSPR-2019 shall be organized by the Department of Electrical Engineering during April 5-7, 2019 at JECRC, Jaipur. ICSPR-2019 aims to bring together the researchers, scientists, engineers, and research scholar in areas of Engineering and Technology, and it provides them a international forum for the dissemination of original research, new ideas and practical development experiences. The conference has the focus on the prime issues in the domain of Electrical Engineering.

Objective of Conference

  • To provide an exceptional platform to the academicians, researchers and students.
  • To meet and discuss the new ideas for startups and help them to get financed from banks.
  • To focus on the recent technological developments in the areas of Electrical Engineering.

Criteria for Publication
The authors are requested to go through the following criteria for publication of their Research papers:

  1. Ranking of papers will be done out of 20 marks on the following basis:
    1. Paper Acceptance out of 10 marks :
      • strongly accepted: (8-10)/10
      • accepted: (6-7)/10
      • weakly accepted: (0-5)/10
    2. Physical Presentation out of 10 marks (as per the session chair(s) decision)
      • strongly presented: (8-10) /10
      • normally presented: (6-7) /10
      • weakly presented: (4-5) /10
      • very weakly presented: (0-3) /10
    *Therefore, the final marking of the paper will be done on the basis of marks obtained out of 20. For example, if a paper is weakly accepted (5 marks), but is strongly presented with satisfying all queries (e.g. 10 out of 10). The final marks will be 5+10 = 15, and hence can get a higher rank.

  2. The best papers evaluated on the basis of their acceptance and physical presentation of the papers will be considered for publication in Springer Nature- BJIT. For this the overall marking of the paper should be between 15-20 marks.

  3. The next best papers in category on the basis of their evaluation , will be published in UGC approved journals.
