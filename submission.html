Paper Submission

Authors are requested to submit their Research Paper at icitda@jecrc.ac.in or easychair. Paper should have an Abstract of 200 words, 4-6 Key Words, Figures, Diagrams, Tables, Graphs and Reference in prescribed Format and Full Length of Paper not more than 6 Pages. Authors have to follow the Dates as mentioned. Papers Selected in ICITDA 2019 will be considered for Publication in UGC approved Journals: Springer Nature [ISSN: 2511-2104 (Print Version); ISSN: 2511-2112 (Electronic Version), http://www.springer.com/computer/journal/41870], IJAC (Journal of Analysis and Computation [ISSN: 0973-28], http://www.ijaconline.com/).

Submissions of high quality papers in all areas of Information Technology and its applications in Engineering. The submissions are handled through the official ICITDA email and Easy Chair:
